CS4MAINLINE

A nonprofit aiming to teach local students and senior citizens in the Philadelphia Mainline area how to code for zero cost.

Basic programming and cybersecurity is a necessary skill to develop in order to thrive in the technologically advancing world - join the CS4Mainline community to get started!

PROGRAMS

Basic Coding

INFO

The Basic Coding Program is a free 10-week program covering basic coding skills starting from Scratch and block-coding to bits of HTML, CSS, as well as introductory Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. First, students will be learning small projects using the Google CSFIRST © curriculum. Once they have a general understanding of block-coding, they will learn website design using aspects of HTML and CSS. Students will get a hands-on experience in the web development realm. Lastly, students will learn about Artificial Intelligence, the future and dangers of AI, as well as how to utilize AI as a tool.

ABOUT US

Financial Officer: Sowmya K.



Hello! I'm Sowmya K,, a current junior residing in the Philadelphia Mainline, with a strong passion for computer science. From web design to AI and machine learning, programming is one of my favorite pastimes. I'm always eager to apply my skills to assist the community, particularly the younger as well as the older generation. As I discovered my love for coding at a young age, I aspire to inspire senior citizens and children to develop an interest in programming as well. Together with Tanvi, our goal is to encourage the Mainline's youth and elderly to pursue programming, and we believe that CS4Mainline is the ideal platform to achieve this.
