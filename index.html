Basic Coding

INFO

The Basic Coding Program is a free 10-week program covering basic coding skills starting from Scratch and block-coding to bits of HTML, CSS, as well as introductory Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. First, students will be learning small projects using the Google CSFIRST Â© curriculum. Cybersecurity

INFO

The CyberSecurity Program is a free 10-week course giving an introduction to cybersecurity and staying safe on the internet. Topics include safe computing, privacy, phishing, scams, mobile computing, password protection and more. The program will also go in depth of new and emerging scams such as deepfakes, AI voices, and AI calls. Students will get a hands-on experience with how to avoid scams and staying safe in the ever-changing digital world.
